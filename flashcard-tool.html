Personal Residence Tax Flashcards

Client-ready answers for home buying, owning, and selling tax questions.

What Is This?

These flashcards simulate real questions your clients will ask about personal residence tax topics. Each card presents a common client question. Your job: answer it like you're sitting across from them in a meeting.

How to Use These Flashcards
1
Read the Question
A client question will appear on the card. Before you flip it, pause. Think about how you'd respond to this client in a real conversation.
2
Formulate Your Answer
Before you flip the card, think through how you'd respond to this client. Formulate your answer in your head as if they're sitting across from you right now. Want to level up? Say it out loud. Speaking your answer builds real confidence and locks in delivery. +XP
3
Flip the Card
Click or tap the card to reveal the answer. You'll see the Key Points to Hit -- the core concepts your answer should cover. Plus, a "How to Say It" script is provided as an example of how you might deliver it to a client in plain language.
4
Reflect and Rate Yourself (1-5)
Compare your answer to the key points. Be honest with yourself. This isn't a test -- it's training for the moments that matter. When you click "Next," you'll rate yourself on a 1-5 scale. Use these questions to guide your rating:
✓ Did I hit the key points?
✓ Was my answer concise?
✓ How long did it take me to respond?
✓ Would the client understand my answer?
✓ Did I sound confident or uncertain?
✓ Did I avoid unnecessary jargon?
